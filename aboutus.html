About Our Blood Bank

Welcome to Mother Teresa Blood Bank

Mother Teresa Blood Bank (A unit of VSES) is a registered NGO society established in the year 2009, with the pledge to help the Blood Needy People & Thalassemia Children’s and Sickle Cell patients. Thalassemia is a Genetic Blood Disorder. Affected patients depend on Regular Blood transfusion for survival.

Mother Teresa Blood Bank is a non-profitable organization that operates independently from government. An essential part of the nation’s healthcare system. Our main aim is conduct Blood Donation Camps and arrange blood and treatment Thalassemia children and add years to their lives. We organize blood donation camps in Hyderabad, Secunderabad and entire Telanagana state. We organize special awareness programs in rural areas of Telanagana state about hygiene and blood donation and Thalassemia. We conduct free medical camps as well in rural areas to prevent health risks.

We supply Blood to below poverty line patients and to the various Govt. hospitalized patients for free of cost. We are best known for ensuring reliable and streamlined access to a stable supply of safe blood and related products wherever they are needed. We supply Whole Blood, Red Blood Packed Cell, Fresh Frozen Plasma, Platelet Rich Plasma, Single Donor Platelets (SDP), Cryoprecipitate, Leuco Depleted Red Blood Cell and Plasmapheresis. We are looking Blood Donation support from all Blood Groups like O, A, B, AB, RH Positive & Negative.

Our Blood Centre performs these tests on Blood & Blood Products.

  • Blood grouping and typing
  • HB levels
  • Blood cross matching
  • Coombs test (updated method using gel technology now routinely done for all blood cross matches)
  • Coombs cross match
  • Tests for uncommon blood cell antigens and antibodies
  • Antibody titre
  • Viral Screening tests for:
    • Hepatitis B and Hepatitis C
    • HIV
    • Venereal diseases
    • Malaria

The above mentioned screening tests are mandatory and have been prescribed under the guidance of the Drug Authority of India which is also the Licensing Authority.
